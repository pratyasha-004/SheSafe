Safety Tips & Guidelines

  • Always be aware of your surroundings and stay alert.
  • Whenever a girl gets into a cab, she should send a picture of the cab's number plate and the driver's name to her parents or someone close.
  • Trust your instincts and avoid dangerous situations.
  • Use your voice confidently to deter attackers.
  • Practice self-defense techniques regularly to stay prepared.
  • Carry personal safety items like a whistle or pepper spray if legal in your area.
  • Try to stay in well-lit and populated areas whenever possible.
  • If attacked, aim for the vulnerable points like the eyes, nose, and groin.
